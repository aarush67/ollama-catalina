Follow these steps to install Ollama on macOS Catalina:

  4. Move the extracted Ollama application to your "Applications" folder for easy access.
  5. If macOS shows a warning when opening the app, go to "System Preferences" > "Security & Privacy" and click "Open Anyway" to bypass the restriction.
